Detailed Description

Repulsion potential with drag, with ε = √(ε₁ ε₂) and σ = (σ₁ + σ₂)/2 Potential is V(r) = ε/n (1 - r/σ)^n, with n = 5/2 usually cutoff at r = σ drag is f = -γv in the normal direction.
